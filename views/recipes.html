Breakfast Burrito

All you need is TJ’s frozen hash browns, eggs, and salsa. Cook, roll, and devour.

Ingredients:
  • 1 frozen hash brown patty
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp salsa
  • 1 tortilla
Instructions:
  1. Cook hash brown according to package instructions.
  2. Scramble the eggs in a pan.
  3. Warm the tortilla and layer hash brown, eggs, and salsa.
  4. Wrap it up and enjoy!

Mini Gnocchi & Pesto

Sauté TJ’s mini gnocchi with a spoon of pesto. That’s it. So good.

Ingredients:
  • 1 bag of TJ’s mini gnocchi
  • 2 tbsp pesto
  • Optional: grated parmesan
Instructions:
  1. Sauté gnocchi in a nonstick pan for 5-7 minutes until golden.
  2. Stir in pesto and cook for another minute.
  3. Top with parmesan if using and serve warm.

Creamy Gochujang Pasta

A spicy, creamy fusion pasta with a kick using TJ’s gochujang and heavy cream.

Ingredients:
  • 1 pack TJ’s pasta (like rigatoni or spaghetti)
  • 1 tbsp gochujang
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Grated parmesan (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Cook pasta and reserve 1/4 cup of the water.
  2. In a pan, melt butter, add gochujang, then stir in cream.
  3. Add pasta and a splash of pasta water. Toss until coated and creamy.
  4. Top with parmesan and serve.

Salmon Rice Bowl

Inspired by Emily Mariko, but simplified with TJ’s frozen salmon and microwave rice.

Ingredients:
  • 1 TJ’s frozen cooked salmon fillet (defrosted)
  • 1 TJ’s microwave jasmine rice
  • 1 tbsp mayo + 1 tsp sriracha (or spicy mayo)
  • Avocado slices
  • Seaweed snacks (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Microwave salmon and rice. Flake salmon into a bowl with rice.
  2. Top with spicy mayo and mix well.
  3. Add avocado on the side. Scoop with seaweed snacks like mini tacos!

Cookie Butter Mug Cake

A quick, warm dessert made in a mug using TJ’s speculoos cookie butter.

Ingredients:
  • 2 tbsp TJ’s cookie butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
Instructions:
  1. Mix everything in a microwave-safe mug until smooth.
  2. Microwave for 60 seconds until puffed and soft.
  3. Top with whipped cream or ice cream if you’re feeling fancy.
